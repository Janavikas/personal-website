Criminology

Criminology is the scientific study of crime and criminal behavior, encompassing both the behavioral and social sciences. It examines the causes, consequences, and prevention of crime, as well as the criminal justice system’s response to it. Criminologists draw upon various disciplines, including sociology, psychology, economics, philosophy, biology, and law, to understand the complex dynamics of crime and its impact on society.

Technology

Technology is all around us, from the smartphones in our pockets to the computers we use at school. But what exactly is technology? Simply put, technology is any tool, system, or invention that helps make our lives easier or solves a problem.Understanding how technology works and how to use it wisely will be important as we continue to live in a world that relies more and more on it.
